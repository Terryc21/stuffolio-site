About Coffee & Code

Coffee & Code LLC · Updated May 2026

Stuffolio is built by an independent one-person software studio. This is the company behind the app.

The studio

Stuffolio is built by Coffee & Code LLC, an independent one-person software studio formed in 2025 and based in Castle Rock, Colorado. Coffee & Code is not venture-backed, not part of a larger company, and not for sale. Coffee & Code exists to ship one thoughtful app at a time and stay close enough to its users to actually answer the email.

The studio was founded by Terry Nyberg. Design, support, writing, the website you’re reading right now — it all comes from the same one person. Read the story of how it started →

One product. On purpose.

Right now there is one product: Stuffolio. That’s not a phase. We don’t plan to grow into a portfolio. The bet is that one app, paid attention to over years instead of weeks, is more useful than five built in a rush.

Why "Coffee & Code"

The name says what the days are made of. Most of the work happens at 4 AM, when the house is quiet and there’s a fresh-ground, fresh-brewed cup of dark roast in reach. The work is slow on purpose. Software gets to be calm if the company behind it isn’t trying to outrun anybody.

What we believe about your stuff

Most home inventory apps are built around acquiring more. We’re not. Stuffolio is built around what’s already in your house — what it does, when it needs attention, and what happens to it eventually. That last part is the part most apps skip, and it’s the reason this one exists.

What we believe about AI

The app uses AI where it earns its keep: identifying a thrift store find (or something you already own) from a photo, estimating its value, locating the user manual, finding DIY repair videos, sourcing replacement parts, and helping you decide whether to keep, sell, or donate it. Outside of that, the app is quiet. Nothing about your inventory is sent anywhere unless you ask for help with a specific item. Stewardship doesn’t need an always-listening assistant; it needs a tool you can put down and pick back up.

What we believe about ownership

Buy once. Keep using it. No accounts to manage, no servers we can shut off. Your data lives on your devices and in your iCloud. If Coffee & Code went away tomorrow, your inventory wouldn’t.
