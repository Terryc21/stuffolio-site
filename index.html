What Nobody Else Does

Every app on this list handles inventory. These are the things only Stuffolio does.

🛡️

Warranty Intelligence

Your fridge has a 3-year warranty. But year 1 is full coverage, years 2-3 are parts only.

Every other app just shows "expires 2027."

Stuffolio tracks coverage phases — and alerts you before they change.

💝

Legacy Wishes

Who gets grandma's ring? The piano? The toolbox?

Other apps don't think about this.

Record who gets what — privately, on your terms, shared when you're ready.

🔎

Stuff Scout AI

What IS this thing? What's it worth?

Other apps make you Google it yourself.

Point your camera. Stuff Scout identifies it, finds the manual, estimates the value, and researches its history.

🔧

Repair, Keep, or Replace?

The dishwasher broke again. Fix it or buy new?

Other apps can't help with this decision.

See repair cost vs. replacement cost, warranty status, your rating, and whether you'd buy this brand again.

⭐

Product Memory

This vacuum broke after 6 months. Never buying this brand again.

Other apps forget your opinions.

Rate every product. Track "Would Buy Again." Your experience becomes your buying guide.

📈

Price Watch

That mixer you paid $350 for costs $550 to replace today.

Insurance wants current replacement cost, not what you paid.

Price Watch tracks how rising prices affect what you own — so you're never under-insured.

Privacy-first by Design

Stuffolio starts quiet on purpose. Internet features, AI assistance, and cloud sync are off by default. You choose what to enable.

🔒 Offline by default
Use the app without signing in or uploading anything. Your data stays on your device.
⚙️ Clear controls
Turn on only what you want, when you want it. No dark patterns or buried settings.
😎 No surveillance vibe
Designed to respect your attention and your data. We never see your inventory.
