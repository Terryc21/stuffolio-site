Yes — Stuffolio does everything you'd expect from a home inventory app. Photos. Notes. Manuals. Receipts. Serial numbers. Warranty details. That's the starting point.

📋

Where most inventory apps stop

Typical inventory apps

  • Focus on capturing more detail
  • Answer: "What do I own?"
  • Stop once it's recorded

They're built for lists.

🧭

Where Stuffolio begins

Stuffolio

  • Tracks each item across its full life — purchase, maintenance, warranty phases, resale, legacy
  • Shows real warranty transitions (full → parts-only → labor-only → expired)
  • Uses AI to identify items, find manuals, and answer questions instantly
  • Prepares documentation for insurance, repair, selling, or donating
  • Helps you decide: maintain it, fix it, insure it, sell it, or pass it on

Stuffolio is built for decisions.

Privacy-first by Design

Stuffolio starts quiet on purpose. Internet features, AI assistance, and cloud sync are off by default. You choose what to enable.

🔒 Offline by default
Use the app without signing in or uploading anything. Your data stays on your device.
⚙️ Clear controls
Turn on only what you want, when you want it. No dark patterns or buried settings.
😎 No surveillance vibe
Designed to respect your attention and your data. We never see your inventory.
🔒

Privacy First
