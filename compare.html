App Profiles

A brief overview of each app's strengths and who it's best suited for.

Stuffolio Warranty-focused

$9.99 one-time + optional AI subscription

A "life-of-stuff" system focused on the full ownership experience: protecting warranties, making repair-vs-replace decisions, and planning for the future.

Strengths

  • Warranty coverage phases (Full ‚Üí Parts Only ‚Üí Limited)

Consider if

  • You want a proven, feature-rich solution

Itemtopia Collections

What Makes Stuffolio Different

We built Stuffolio because existing apps treated inventory as a static catalog. We wanted to track the full life of each item ‚Äî from purchase through ownership to eventual disposition. Here's what that means in practice:

Warranty Coverage Phases

Real warranties change over time. A 5-year appliance warranty might offer full coverage in year 1, parts-only in years 2-3, and limited coverage in years 4-5. Stuffolio tracks these transitions and notifies you before coverage changes ‚Äî so you know what's actually covered right now, not just when the warranty expires.

Apple Wallet Warranty Cards

When you're at a repair shop, fumbling through an app to prove warranty coverage is frustrating. Stuffolio generates Apple Wallet passes for your warranties ‚Äî one tap from your Lock Screen shows proof of coverage. We haven't found another home inventory app that does this.

The Full Support Experience

Dealing with warranty claims involves more than tracking dates. Stuffolio logs RMA numbers, claim status, and shipping info. It also records customer support interactions ‚Äî rep names, case numbers, what was promised, and follow-up dates. When you're on your third call to customer service, this context is invaluable.

Product Memory

When your blender breaks after 8 years, you probably can't remember if the brand was any good. Stuffolio's product ratings and "Would Buy Again" tracking build a personal database of your experiences. The "Repair, Keep, or Replace?" tool uses this history ‚Äî alongside repair costs and remaining lifespan ‚Äî to help you decide what to do next.

Privacy Model

Your inventory syncs through your personal iCloud account ‚Äî we never see your data. This is similar to Under My Roof's approach, and different from apps that sync through company servers. For AI features, we send only the data needed for that specific query, and we don't use your data to train models.

Where Others Excel

In the interest of fairness, here's where some competitors have advantages:

  • Under My Roof has 17+ years of development and is an App Store Editors' Choice. Its supply inventory with low-stock alerts and 3D room scanning are features Stuffolio doesn't offer.
  • Itemtopia can create instant webpages to sell items ‚Äî great for collectors who frequently buy and sell. Its Vision Pro support is ahead of most apps.
  • Sortly has web access and team collaboration features that make it better suited for businesses and shared workspaces.
  • Everspruce offers up to 3 additional users free ‚Äî generous for households that don't want per-seat pricing.
  • Memento Database offers unmatched flexibility ‚Äî if you want complete control over data structure and are comfortable with databases, it's hard to beat.

The "best" app depends entirely on your priorities. We think Stuffolio is the best choice for people who take warranties seriously and want to track the full ownership experience ‚Äî but we're obviously biased. Try a few apps and see what feels right for your workflow.
