Support

We’ll help you get your stuff together. (No judgment. We all have too much.)

Contact Support: support@stuffolio.app
Please include: device type (iPhone/iPad/Mac), iOS/macOS version, and what you were trying to do.

Common Questions

  • Do I need receipts? No. Add what you know now.
  • Can I use AI? Yes, but it’s optional and only runs when you tap an AI action.
  • Can I export? If your build supports export, you’ll find it in the app’s export/share area.

Troubleshooting

  • Sync not updating? Try force-quitting and reopening, then check connectivity.
  • AI results not loading? Confirm you’re online, then try again.
  • Crashes? Email us the steps to reproduce.

Feature Requests

Send ideas to support@stuffolio.app. Tell us what problem you’re solving (e.g., “insurance claim inventory,” “donation tracking,” “warranty reminders”).
