üé¨ Try These Scenarios

Real-world tasks that naturally exercise multiple features.

The New Purchase

You just bought a blender. Add it to Stuffolio with a photo, set the warranty, and rate it.

Exercises: Quick Add, photo capture, warranty tracking, ratings

The Mystery Item

Find something in your house you're not sure about. Use Stuff Scout to identify it and add it to your inventory.

Exercises: AI identification, auto-fill, editing suggestions

The Shared Appliance

Add your washing machine and share it with a family member so you both can track its maintenance.

Exercises: Family sharing, maintenance suggestions, permissions

The Declutter Decision

Pick something you might donate. Use the Donate vs. Sell Advisor to see which option is better financially.

Exercises: AI advisor, value estimation, disposal tracking

The Multi-Device Edit

Start editing an item on your iPhone, then continue on your iPad or Mac. Try editing the same field on two devices at once.

Exercises: iCloud sync, Handoff, conflict resolution
