üí≥ Apple Wallet Warranty Cards NEW

Add a product with a warranty date, then look for the Apple Wallet section. Tap "Add to Wallet" to create a warranty pass you can access from your lock screen.

Why it matters: First release of Wallet integration ‚Äî does the pass look right? Is the status color correct?

AppleCare+ Tracking NEW

Add an Apple product (iPhone, iPad, Mac, etc.) and save it. You should see a prompt asking about AppleCare+. Check for the blue Care+ badge in product lists.

Why it matters: Beta tester feedback said AppleCare was hard to find ‚Äî does this help?

üì∏ Photo Selection Improvements NEW

Add a product and tap "Add Photo" in Quick Start. After selecting a photo, you'll see quality feedback and options to extract details via OCR or suggest a category.

Why it matters: New photo assessment UI ‚Äî is the quality feedback helpful? Does OCR extract correctly?

üë®‚Äçüë©‚Äçüëß‚Äçüë¶ Family Sharing Sprint 13

üëÜ Tappable Detail Fields NEW

In product detail view, tap any field (name, manufacturer, model, serial number, etc.) to edit it directly. No need to tap the Edit button first.

Why it matters: Streamlined editing workflow ‚Äî does tapping feel natural and responsive?

üîÑ Two-Tier Condition Picker NEW

When setting product condition, select "Used" or "Refurbished" to reveal sub-conditions (Like New, Good, Fair, Poor for Used; Certified, Seller, Other for Refurbished).

Why it matters: More granular condition tracking ‚Äî is the two-tier picker intuitive?

üì∑ Quick Start Card Improvements NEW

The Quick Start card now groups actions: Add Photos (Scan Barcode, Take Photo, Choose Photo, Paste for OCR, Drag & Drop) and Scan for Details (Scan Receipt, Scan Label). Tap the ? icon for built-in help.

Why it matters: Better organization ‚Äî are the groupings clear? Does the help button work?

üì∏ Take Photo (Direct Camera) NEW

On iPhone, tap "Take Photo" in Quick Start to open the camera directly and capture a product photo without going through the photo library.

Why it matters: Faster photo capture ‚Äî does the camera open reliably?

üìã Clipboard Preview Thumbnail NEW

When you have an image copied to your clipboard, "Paste for OCR" shows a small thumbnail preview of what will be pasted.

Why it matters: Visual confirmation before pasting ‚Äî does the thumbnail display correctly?

üñºÔ∏è Drag & Drop Zone (iPad) NEW

On iPad in Split View or Slide Over, drag images from other apps into the dedicated drop zone in Quick Start.

Why it matters: iPad multitasking workflow ‚Äî does drag & drop work smoothly?

üìÇ Images & Documents Auto-Expand NEW

When editing a product that has photos or documents attached, those sections now auto-expand so you can see the content immediately.

Why it matters: Better visibility ‚Äî does auto-expand feel helpful or annoying?

üè∑Ô∏è Multi-Category Support NEW

Products can now belong to multiple categories (up to 5). When adding or editing a product, use the new chip-based category picker to select multiple categories. Items appear in all applicable groups in Gallery View.

Why it matters: Many items fit multiple categories ‚Äî is the picker intuitive? Do items show up correctly in filtered views?

üç≥ New Product Categories NEW

Three new categories added: Kitchen & Dining (cookware, appliances, dinnerware), Personal Care (grooming, beauty, hygiene products), and Antique/Collectable (vintage items, collectibles, heirlooms).

Why it matters: Better organization ‚Äî do these categories cover items you couldn't categorize before?

üé¨ Try These Scenarios

Exercises: AI advisor, value estimation, disposal tracking

Exercises: iCloud sync, Handoff, conflict resolution
