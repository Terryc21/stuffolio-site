What is Stuffolio?

Stuffolio helps you track everything you own ‚Äî warranties, purchase dates, manuals, and more. Never lose track of when a warranty expires or where you put that receipt again.

Getting Started (5 Minutes)

Step 1: Add Your First Product

  1. Open Stuffolio
  2. Tap the + button (bottom right on iPhone, toolbar on iPad/Mac)
  3. Choose your situation:
    • Something I just got ‚Äî New purchases with warranties
    • Something I already have ‚Äî Existing items (photo-first, focuses on current value)
    • Multiple identical items ‚Äî Batch entry for collections
    • Record a donation ‚Äî Track charitable donations
    • Use Stuff Scout AI ‚Äî Let AI identify items from photos
  4. Enter the Product Name (required)
  5. Add optional details:
    • Manufacturer ‚Äî Who made it
    • Model Number ‚Äî Found on product label
    • Serial Number ‚Äî For warranty claims
    • Purchase Date ‚Äî When you bought it (use "Approximate" if unsure)
    • Purchase Price or Current Value ‚Äî What you paid or what it's worth today
    • Warranty Expiration ‚Äî When coverage ends
  6. Tap Save
Pro Tip: Use "Something I already have" for items without receipts ‚Äî it starts with a photo and emphasizes current market value for insurance purposes.

The Dashboard shows at-a-glance:

  • Recently Added ‚Äî Your latest additions

Key Features

My Products

Browse, search, and filter all your products. Tap any product to view details, edit, or access AI assistance.

Smart Scanner

Scan barcodes to auto-fill product information:

  1. Go to Actions > Smart Scanner
  2. Point camera at UPC barcode
  3. Review auto-detected product info
  4. Save to your inventory

Stuff Scout (AI Identification)

Identify antiques, collectibles, and unknown items:

  1. Go to Actions > Stuff Scout
  2. Take a photo of the item
  3. AI analyzes and provides identification, era, maker, and estimated value
  4. If the results aren't quite right, tap Not quite right? to provide hints and re-analyze
  5. Results automatically save to Recent Scans ‚Äî view anytime in Scout History
Pro Tip: Your last 25 scans are auto-saved. Swipe right on any recent scan to bookmark it permanently. Bookmarks never expire.

Photo Quick Add ("Snap & Done")

The fastest way to add items ‚Äî let AI do the typing:

  1. Tap + ‚Üí Use Photo Quick Add
  2. Choose photo type:
    • Product ‚Äî photo of the item itself
    • Nameplate/Label ‚Äî close-up of brand/model/serial info
    • Receipt ‚Äî purchase receipt with date, store, price
    • Packaging ‚Äî box with product details
  3. Snap the photo (or choose from library)
  4. AI analyzes and auto-fills form fields
  5. Review, edit if needed, tap Save
Pro Tip: You can take multiple photo types in one session ‚Äî product + nameplate + receipt. AI combines all the info into one complete item record.

Voice Input

Add items hands-free with natural language:

  1. Tap the microphone button in Quick Start
  2. Grant permissions (first time only)
  3. Speak naturally: "Add coffee maker to kitchen, manufacturer Cuisinart, price 99 dollars"
  4. Review parsed fields in the preview
  5. Tap Use This Input to fill the form

Recognized: Title, room, manufacturer, price, model number, serial number, and category keywords.

Privacy: Voice recognition uses on-device processing (iOS 17+). Your voice stays on your device.

Rate AI Responses

After using AI features (Stuff Scout, Product Assistant, Quick Add), you'll see thumbs up/down buttons:

  • Thumbs Up ‚Äî Response was helpful, accurate, or useful
  • Thumbs Down ‚Äî Response was off-target, wrong, or confusing
  • Skip ‚Äî Don't want to rate? Just close and move on

Your feedback helps improve AI accuracy for everyone. Ratings are anonymous and optional.

Scout Refinement (Re-Analyze Existing Items)

Already have an item in your inventory? Re-analyze it with Stuff Scout:

  1. Open the item and tap Edit
  2. Expand the Stuff Scout section
  3. Tap Analyze with Stuff Scout
  4. Scout analyzes the item's photos and shows a comparison
  5. Review current values vs. Scout's findings side-by-side
  6. Check boxes for fields you want to update
  7. Tap Apply Selected to update your item
Pro Tip: Scout Refinement is perfect for items you added quickly ‚Äî add details later without starting over.

Receipt Pairing

Combine product identification with purchase details in one flow:

  1. After Stuff Scout identifies your product, tap Add to Inventory
  2. In the preview sheet, tap Add Receipt Photo
  3. Capture or select a photo of your receipt
  4. Store name, date, and price auto-fill from the receipt
  5. Review and save ‚Äî complete record with no manual typing

AI Product Assistant

Get instant help for any product:

  1. Open a product's detail view
  2. Tap AI Assistant
  3. Choose from: Find Manual, Find Warranty Info, Find Parts, Troubleshoot Problems, Maintenance Tips, How-To Videos

Offline Access: All AI responses are cached for 30 days. Access previous answers without internet ‚Äî perfect for travel or limited connectivity.

Recall Alerts

Check if any of your products have been recalled:

  1. Go to Insights > Recall Check
  2. Tap Check All Products to scan your inventory
  3. View matching recalls from the CPSC database
  4. Tap any recall for details and remedy instructions

Tip: Use accurate product names and include manufacturers for better matching. Tap Learn more for tips.

Product Ratings

When something wears out or breaks, you'll want to know whether to buy the same brand again or switch. Product Ratings helps you track your experience so you can make smarter replacement decisions:

  1. Open any product's detail view
  2. Tap Your Rating section
  3. Set 1-5 stars and "Would Buy Again" preference
  4. Add notes about your experience
  5. View your satisfaction insights in Insights > Rating Insights

Smart Maintenance Suggestions

Get category-specific maintenance recommendations:

  1. Open a product's detail view
  2. Scroll to Maintenance section
  3. Tap Get Suggestions
  4. Add suggested tasks to your schedule

Maintenance Task Reminders

Track multiple maintenance tasks per item with customizable schedules:

  1. Open item ‚Üí Maintenance tab
  2. Tap Add Maintenance Task
  3. Enter task name (e.g., "Replace water filter")
  4. Set interval: manual (free) or tap AI Suggest (uses 1 query)
  5. Add optional notes
  6. Tap Add to save

You'll get notifications when tasks are due. Mark complete to reset the timer.

Pro Tip: Add multiple tasks per item ‚Äî e.g., refrigerator: water filter (6 months), coil cleaning (12 months), door seal check (24 months).

Family/Household Sharing

Share your inventory with family members:

  1. Go to Settings > Family Sharing
  2. Tap Create Household (or join an existing one)
  3. Invite members by email
  4. Share items by toggling Share with Household on any product

Legacy Wishes (Optional)

Record who should receive your belongings:

  1. Enable in Settings > Legacy Planning
  2. Add Recipients (family, friends, charities)
  3. Assign items to recipients from any product's detail view or from the Edit screen
  4. Share as PDF or let family view in-app
Pro Tip: You can now assign Legacy Wishes directly from the Edit Item view ‚Äî look for the "Legacy Wishes" section while editing any item.
Note: This is not a legal will ‚Äî just a way to document your preferences. Consult an attorney for legally binding arrangements.
