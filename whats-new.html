Version 1.0 (Build 33)

April 21, 2026 Beta

A redesigned Home tab on iPhone, a much smarter Stuff Scout, a new Recreation category for boats and RVs, and dozens of fixes that protect your data.

Highlight

A redesigned Home tab with Three Phases -- The iPhone Home tab is now organized around how you actually use your stuff: Acquire, Maintain, and Move On. The right tools are in reach at the right moment.

New

  • Home Tab: Three Phases -- A reorganized iPhone Home tab with sections for acquiring new things, maintaining what you own, and moving things on when you're ready
  • Borrowed & Lent entry point -- A dedicated way to see what you've lent out and what you've borrowed, right from the Home tab
  • Recreation category -- Track boats, RVs, ATVs, and other recreational vehicles as a first-class category
  • Scout: "Is this right?" -- After every scan, Scout asks you to confirm, add more context, or try again. No more accepting a wrong answer because the app stopped asking.
  • Scout: Tell your story first -- Describe what you know in plain language ("This belonged to my grandfather, might be from the 1940s"). Structured fields are optional extras.
  • Scout takes your notes seriously -- but not at face value -- Specific facts get verified against the photo. Speculative guesses are treated as hypotheses to investigate, not facts to accept.
  • Scout: The right marketplace for the item -- Price verification goes to the places that actually sell your kind of item. Instruments check Reverb. Collectibles check eBay.
  • Scout: Where to learn more -- Scout suggests specific forums, reference sites, and YouTube channels for the kind of item you're looking at.
  • Scout: See what each round added -- A history of how your added details improved the identification and valuation.
  • Activity Timeline on Mac -- A new card on the Mac Dashboard showing your recent activity at a glance.
  • Item thumbnails in Legacy Wishes -- The items you're passing along are now pictured when you create a wish.
  • Tappable "--" fields -- Blank fields in the Item detail now invite you to fill them in instead of just showing a dash.
  • Coverage warnings -- When an item's replacement cost has grown stale, Stuffolio tells you -- so you're not under-insured without realizing.
  • Donation documentation prompts -- For donations above the IRS documentation threshold, Stuffolio prompts for the details the IRS will want.

Improvements

  • A cleaner Item detail view -- Facts like serial numbers and purchase dates appear as quick, scannable rows. Status cards (warranty, recalls) stay prominent for the things you actually need to notice.
  • Clearer warranty tracking -- A new progress bar, coverage-phase hints, a redesigned support section, and clearer price labels.
  • Easier maintenance setup -- The setup sheet is redesigned and the category selection bug is fixed.
  • More accurate Price Watch -- Baselines no longer drift. Scout valuations now flow to Price Watch reliably.
  • Prices in your local format -- The Add and Edit forms now format prices the way your region does.
  • Dashboard stat cards easier to tell apart -- Adjacent stats no longer share the same color.
  • Accessibility improvements -- Better color contrast throughout, graceful handling of very long text, and respect for "Reduce Transparency."
  • Unsaved notes are protected -- Dismissing the notes editor with unsaved changes now prompts you to confirm.
  • "Clear all notifications" asks first -- A confirmation prompt before wiping your notification list.

Bug Fixes

  • Hidden fields now appear -- Several fields that you could fill in during Add or Edit but never saw on the Item detail page are now visible.
  • Tapping an item on iPhone opens it -- In gallery and card layouts, tapping an item now reliably takes you to its detail view.
  • Rotating during a Scout scan is safe -- Scout no longer dismisses when you rotate your iPhone.
  • Scout history loads completely -- Past scans now recall their full results, including all text sections and share text.
  • Scout recognizes maker's marks -- A parsing issue with maker's marks is fixed. Sort order restored. Vehicle valuations improved.
  • Legacy Wishes is more reliable -- A crash when viewing shared Legacy data is fixed. When sync fails, you'll see a clear error instead of silence.
  • Beneficiary form protects your work -- If you close the form with unsaved recipient fields, you're now prompted to confirm.
  • Form drafts keep all your fields -- Room, condition, quantity, and how-you-got-it now all save in a draft.
  • CSV import doesn't freeze the app -- Import runs in the background with progress and a cancel button. Seller names import in their consistent form.
  • Safer backups -- A backup restore crash is fixed. Conflict resolution no longer loses your data. Donated items with attached files delete cleanly.
  • No more freeze on the Item detail -- A freeze and crash that some users saw on their iPhone is resolved. Launch feels faster.
  • macOS notes sheet no longer flickers.

Version 1.0 (Build 30)

March 26, 2026 Beta

Record how you got each item, share Scout reports, better Mac readability, and much more reliable sync across devices.

Highlight

Acquisition Type Tracking -- Record how you got each item: Purchased New, Purchased Used, Inherited, Gifted, or Found. Form labels adapt automatically so gift entries hide the price field and inherited items show provenance options.

New

  • Acquisition Types -- 6 ways to record how you got an item, with form labels that adapt to each type
  • Share Scout Reports -- Save and share Stuff Scout identification results with copy buttons on each section
  • Better Mac Readability -- Larger fonts on Mac so you can read comfortably at desktop distance
  • Cost Basis Tracking -- Track additional costs beyond purchase price and see your total investment
  • Coverage Insights -- See if your warranty coverage amount has fallen behind replacement costs

Improvements

  • Better Sync -- More of your data now syncs across iPhone, iPad, and Mac, including extended warranties and maintenance tasks
  • Better Import/Export -- CSV files now carry more of your data when moving between apps
  • Widget Links -- Tapping widgets now takes you to the right place in the app
  • Visual Refresh -- Bolder, more colorful cards and sections throughout the app
  • Better Color Accessibility -- Improved contrast for users with color vision differences
  • Scout Values -- Value estimates from Stuff Scout now automatically appear in Price Watch

Bug Fixes

  • Sync Reliability -- Edits now propagate more reliably between devices
  • Backup Safety -- Improved data preservation when restoring from backups
  • iPhone Navigation -- Fixed some sections not opening correctly on iPhone
  • Long-Term Stability -- Fixed issues that could appear after using the app for extended periods
