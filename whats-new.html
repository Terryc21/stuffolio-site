Version 1.0 (Build 30)

March 26, 2026 Beta

Record how you got each item, share Scout reports, better Mac readability, and much more reliable sync across devices.

Highlight

Acquisition Type Tracking -- Record how you got each item: Purchased New, Purchased Used, Inherited, Gifted, or Found. Form labels adapt automatically so gift entries hide the price field and inherited items show provenance options.

New

  • Acquisition Types -- 6 ways to record how you got an item, with form labels that adapt to each type
  • Share Scout Reports -- Save and share Stuff Scout identification results with copy buttons on each section
  • Better Mac Readability -- Larger fonts on Mac so you can read comfortably at desktop distance
  • Cost Basis Tracking -- Track additional costs beyond purchase price and see your total investment
  • Coverage Insights -- See if your warranty coverage amount has fallen behind replacement costs

Improvements

  • Better Sync -- More of your data now syncs across iPhone, iPad, and Mac, including extended warranties and maintenance tasks
  • Better Import/Export -- CSV files now carry more of your data when moving between apps
  • Widget Links -- Tapping widgets now takes you to the right place in the app
  • Visual Refresh -- Bolder, more colorful cards and sections throughout the app
  • Better Color Accessibility -- Improved contrast for users with color vision differences
  • Scout Values -- Value estimates from Stuff Scout now automatically appear in Price Watch

Bug Fixes

  • Sync Reliability -- Edits now propagate more reliably between devices
  • Backup Safety -- Improved data preservation when restoring from backups
  • iPhone Navigation -- Fixed some sections not opening correctly on iPhone
  • Long-Term Stability -- Fixed issues that could appear after using the app for extended periods

Version 1.0 (Build 29)

March 20, 2026 Beta

Smarter product replacement recommendations and a better photo-adding experience.

Highlight

Smarter Replacement Suggestions -- Repair, Keep, or Replace now shows alternative product recommendations right in the decision view, so you can research options without leaving the screen.

New

  • Inline Alternatives -- See replacement product suggestions while deciding whether to repair, keep, or replace an item

Improvements

  • Photo Collection -- Clearer guidance when adding multiple photos, with a count badge and contextual button labels
  • Recall History -- Previously checked recall results are now saved for offline reference
  • Accessibility -- Fixed buttons going off-screen at large text sizes
  • Delete Confirmations -- Added visible delete buttons with safety confirmations in more places

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed second photo not being added when choosing from library
  • Improved Spotlight search performance
  • Fixed wrong item opening when using Handoff between devices
  • Fixed deep links sometimes not opening the correct screen

Version 1.0 (Build 26)

March 2, 2026 Beta

Dashboard redesign, accessibility improvements, and pre-release polish.

New

  • Dashboard Redesign -- Fresh layout with a search prompt and Customize shortcut
  • Smarter Repair Advisor -- Uses your actual item data for more accurate Repair, Keep, or Replace recommendations
  • Direct AppleCare+ Editing -- Edit AppleCare+ details without opening the full item editor
  • iCloud Sync in Empty State -- One-tap option to turn on iCloud Sync when your inventory is empty

Improvements

  • Color Accessibility -- Distinct, solid-color icons throughout the app for better visibility
  • Dynamic Type -- All text respects your preferred text size, from smallest to largest
  • Go To Navigation -- More features registered and every result opens its destination directly
  • Mac Theme Toggle -- Light/Dark switch available directly in the Dashboard header on Mac
  • Faster Photo Loading -- Photos load in the background for smoother scrolling
  • Better iCloud Sync -- Improved duplicate prevention and photo syncing

Bug Fixes

  • Improved bulk delete recovery to prevent data loss on interruption
  • Fixed crashes when deleting items with photos or attachments
  • Resolved duplicate items appearing on other devices after sync
  • Fixed dismiss button not responding after viewing Stuff Scout results

Version 1.0 (Build 25)

February 20, 2026 Beta

See your original Scout results from any item, simpler onboarding, and new bulk tools.

New

  • View Scout Results -- Items added via Stuff Scout now show a button to revisit the original identification
  • Siri Shortcuts -- 7 voice commands including "What's my inventory worth?" and "Show expiring warranties"
  • Simpler Onboarding -- 3 simple toggles to set up your experience
  • Encrypted Backups -- Optional password protection for your backup files
  • Scan Depth Options -- Choose Quick ID, With Pricing, or Full Appraisal when scanning items
  • Bulk Tools -- Data Cleanup, Batch Categorize, and Bulk Price Update

Version 1.0 (Build 21)

January 30, 2026 Beta

Household Sharing and Price Watch.

Highlight

Household Sharing -- Create a household and invite family members. Share appliances, vehicles, and electronics with real-time sync. See who added each item and resolve conflicts when edits overlap.

Also New

Price Watch -- Track current market prices for your items. See how replacement costs change over time, view price history, and get alerts when prices drop.

New

  • Household Sharing -- Invite family with Viewer, Editor, or Admin permissions
  • Price Watch -- Monitor replacement costs from multiple sources
  • Component Warranties -- Track different warranty periods for different parts of a product
  • Legacy Wishes Sharing -- Share your Legacy Wishes with family via iCloud
  • Smart Auto-Fill -- AI-discovered manufacturer websites and support pages saved to your items with one tap
